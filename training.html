    "Muscle gain is based on three factors: diet, training, recovery."


    Diet

    Muscle growth occurs with a calorie surplus or a positive energy balance. To get the required muscle mass we must follow a high-calorie diet so that the source of these calories is from a healthy food that depends on protein, carbohydrates, healthy fats, salts and vitamins and do not forget about water.

    How can I develop a high-calorie diet?


    First: Calculate my daily caloric needs;

    Second: After calculating the daily caloric needs, some calories must be added above our daily needs, so that the caloric surplus is 5-10%, which is more than the amount of calories in maintenance, which drives the growth of muscle fibers in a healthy way and low fat percentage;

    Example: Your daily needs are 2500 kcal, so you should only add 125 to 250 kcal per day.

    Third: Divide your nutritional needs into five or six meals in the form of small meals.

    Training program to increase muscle mass


    The program aims to gain muscle mass for muscle growth and the use of so-called "explosive" strength fibers. This is why it is based on:

    • 80% bodybuilding;
    • 20% Cardio Exercise (Cardio can be dispensed for ectomorph);
    • Weights: 50% heavy weights and 50% medium weights;
    • The frequency of training is between 3 to 4 training sessions per week;
    • Duration of training: 1 hour training time.

    As for the big muscle exercise, it is divided into two parts:

    First, the big muscle exercise for beginners and the exercise is twice a week by four to five exercises at a time, and the repetitions are from 10 to 12 repetitions, after performing the general exercise that performs the preparation and alerting of the muscles and joints to bear pregnancy Reality then.
    And the beginner player takes into account the shape of the fist and the posture of the exercise, through the specialized trainer, and also takes into account the intervals between the interstitials that do not exceed one to two and a half minutes, and the rest periods between exercises do not exceed two minutes.

    Second: The big muscle exercises for advanced players, that is, whoever practiced the game more than two continuous years without interruption except for positive rest, so the big muscle exercise is twice a week at 5 to 6 exercises at one time, and the man's muscle can reach 7 exercises as it is one of the largest muscles Size in the body, as well as help increase muscle mass hormones, by four groups for each exercise in 8 to 10 reps.

    As for small muscle exercises, first: For beginner players, small muscles can be exercised twice a week at 3 to 4 exercises at a time, and that is three groups in 10 to 12 iterations.
    As for the advanced players, the small muscle exercise is twice a week, with 4 exercises each time, and that is four groups in 8 to 10 reps,and the rest between the groups between 30 seconds to one minute, and between exercises a minute and a half, and in general the player must know that the training dose does not exceed sixty minutes, because at this time the best level of the hormone responsible for muscle building.
    After sixty minutes, the body begins to secrete the hormone cortisol, which is responsible for the destruction of muscles, and this is negative for the player. Tripps and the high chest are driving and auxiliary muscles. This can lead to injuries.

    The player must know that the period of recovery of the large muscle reaches 72 hours and the small muscle reaches 48 hours.


    Training tips

    The muscle mass increases with weight training exercises or the so-called negative movement, so that the weight drop is slower than the lifting then the pressure is focused on the muscle, in the event of weight gain as much as possible, because light weights increase the size of the muscles slowly, but increase the weight while reducing repetitions Exercise gains a significant increase in muscle mass quickly.

    Changing the exercise pattern when returning to the muscle amplification again, as using the same exercise method and the same weights leads to the stability of the muscle mass, while it is possible to return once every month or six weeks to the same muscle with a different exercise pattern.

    Training with free weights, and focusing on compound exercises, because the devices train specific parts of the muscle, so the constant is training using the bar and dumbbells, then exercising with the machines next.


    How to determine the appropriate weights for carrying weights?


    By measuring the largest weight you can carry 1RM In order to achieve noticeable results from your workouts, it is preferred that the weights you carry are within 55% to 85% of the largest weight that you can carry once for a correct technique of 1 RM. The percentage you choose from your 1RM depends on your training goals, and generally:

    • High repetitions (more than 15) and with weights ranging from 55-65% of the 1RM value, are used to increase muscle durability.
    • Average frequencies (about 10) and using weights around 75% of 1RM are considered excellent for increasing muscle size.
    • Low iterations (less than 6) and weights around 80% of the 1RM are excellent for increasing muscle strength.
    • Very few iterations (1-2) with weights ranging from 85-95% of the 1RM value, used by professional heavyweights.
